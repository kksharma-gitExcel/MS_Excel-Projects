List Email Details Within Date Range

Purpose:
Extracts emails from Outlook Inbox received within a user-defined date range and displays the details in Excel (Sheet1).

Key Features:

* Validates dates from cells "K1" and "K2".
* Connects to Outlook and filters emails by received date.
* Lists sender name, email, subject, CC, received time, and attachment count.
* Applies header formatting, borders, and date formatting.
* Alerts user for invalid dates or if no emails are found.
